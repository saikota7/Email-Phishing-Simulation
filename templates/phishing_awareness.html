Phishing Awareness

Phishing is a common cyber threat. Here are some tips to stay safe:

  • Verify the sender's email address before clicking on any links.
  • Avoid clicking on links or downloading attachments from unknown or suspicious emails.
  • Check for spelling and grammar errors in emails, as phishing emails often contain these mistakes.

Examples of common phishing tactics:

Example 1: Fake Login Page

Phishers may create fake login pages to steal your credentials. Always verify the URL before entering your login details.

Tip: Hover over links to view the actual URL before clicking.

Example 2: Urgent Requests

Be cautious of emails claiming urgent action is required. Phishers often use urgency to manipulate users into providing sensitive information.

Tip: Verify urgent requests by contacting the sender through a trusted method.

Example 3: Prize Winner Scam

Watch out for emails claiming you've won a prize or lottery that you never entered. Avoid providing personal information or payment.

Tip: Verify such claims with the official organization through their official channels.

Example 4: Phishing Links in Text

Phishers might use misleading text to make users click on malicious links. Always check the actual destination of links before clicking.

Tip: Hover over links to preview the URL before clicking.

Stay informed and report any suspicious emails to your IT department.
